Fact or Fiction 2021



Ghazaleh has also participated in many exhibitions at Melbourne Polytechnic and has won the Fact or Fiction Award for 2021 on her portrait photograph as shown on the right.



The photo was taken within a studio and the hair, pose, and makeup was inspired by well-known Marilyn Monroe. Her work was well acknowledged, especially due to its magnificence considering covid-19 lockdowns and won the grand prize.
